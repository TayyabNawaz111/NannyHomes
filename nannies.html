Trusted Nanny Services by Nannyhomes

At Nannyhomes, we understand that trusting someone with your child is one of the most important decisions a parent can make. That’s why we work hard to connect you with reliable, caring and experienced nannies who are fully vetted and background-checked.

Whether you need full-time, part-time, weekend or after-school care, our nanny services are flexible and designed around your family’s routine. Each nanny in our network is trained to engage children with age-appropriate activities, maintain routines and ensure a safe, nurturing environment within your home.

We proudly serve families across Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, and other major cities in Pakistan. Booking a nanny with Nannyhomes is simple: fill out a quick form, tell us your requirements and we’ll match you with the right person.

Let us take the stress out of childcare so you can focus on what matters most.

Why Choose Nannyhomes?

  • Verified background checks and interviews
  • Flexible scheduling (full-time, part-time, weekends)
  • Child-first approach with play, learning, and care
  • Emergency replacement support if needed

With Nannyhomes, you get all the benefits of personalized in-home care with the trust and transparency of a professional platform.
