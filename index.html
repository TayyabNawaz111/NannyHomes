Nannyhomes vs. Daycare Centers

Feature Nannyhomes Daycare Centers
Care Environment Your home (familiar, safe, personal) Shared facility with many children
Child-to-Caregiver Ratio 1:1 or 1:2 personalized care Often high child-to-staff ratio
Flexibility Fully tailored to your schedule Fixed timings
Health & Hygiene Controlled by your household standards Exposure to multiple children & germs
Emotional Bond Strong, consistent caregiver relationship Rotating staff and larger groups
Travel Hassles None Daily pickup and drop-off required
Cost Efficiency (per child) Competitive for multiple children Can become costly with added services
